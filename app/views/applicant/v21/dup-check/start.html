Apply for an NHS Care ID

You must complete your application by Friday 15 December 2023 5pm.

How to apply

  1. Enter your personal details

    You will need your name, date of birth and National Insurance number.

  2. Find out what ID documents you need to provide

    We will tell you what photo IDs and proof of address document you need to provide.

  3. Upload a professional photo of your face

    The photo you upload will appear on your smartcard (if applicable) and your NHS Care ID profile.

    Smartcard photo guidelines

    Your photo must:

    • be clear and in focus
    • be taken against a plain, light coloured background
    • show you facing forwards and looking straight at the camera
    • show you with a plain expression and your mouth closed
    • have your head and shoulders visible
    • only include a head covering for religious or medical reasons
    • be suitable for a work setting

  4. Take photos of your ID documents using your smartphone

    You will need a smartphone with a camera and the original ID documents we have told you to provide.

    I cannot use a smartphone to take photos and scan my face.

  5. Take a photo of your face using your smartphone

    This will be used to match you to the ID documents you have provided.

You can use the link in your invite email to return to your application at another time.
